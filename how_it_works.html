Service Consumer


We will provide matching services for businesses and individuals looking to hire talent or organisations for permanent or fixed term contracts with optimised algorithms. They will also be provided with the option to place their work or service contracts to allow for more open and fair competitions. Bids will be supported with advanced auction theory to tailor to the needs of involved parties. We will also create unique payment schedules where service consumers can request for delayed payment. We will facilitate these promissory notes or other financial instruments agreed upon by the parties involved. We will design economic incentives and frameworks which will aid these parties to agree to terms and conditions which would be mutually beneficial to all those involved. We plan to embed these contracts in blockchain technology which will enhance trust and security.

Service Provider


Businesses and individuals looking to sell their talents or services would be able to advertise and showcase their offerings with a transparent rating system embedded in blockchain to ensure that we are not manipulating any reviews or ratings. The service provider will benefit because we will not charge them a transaction fee whenever they use our systems. Our systems will create more jobs or service contract opportunities for these providers as we will be asking them to delay receiving payment. We will ensure that they earn interest on these delayed payments or have convertible equity arrangements. We will also create a secondary market for these service providers so that they could sell these contracts and get their payments immediately.

Investor


We will create a secondary market for individuals or institutions that would like to invest in credit arrangements between our service providers and service consumers. These investors would be allowed to trade these contracts as they please. Once the loan is paid back, we will allow the investor to either keep this contract as a cryptocurrency with the principal plus interest held in our vaults or they can return this contract and collect their earnings. Governments seeking to stimulate their labour markets without guaranteeing furlough payments can instead buy these contracts and allow business to pay back these labour loan agreements when they can. Retail investors can buy these contracts and earn interest, in a low interest world, this would beat keeping money in a savings account while being less risky than investing in the stock market. We will eventually expand our investment offerings by allowing these investors to crowdfund projects including infrastructure and housing.
